The Four Audits

Each audit targets a distinct phase of how clients discover, evaluate, and hire a law firm. Run one. Run all four. Find out where you're bleeding.

01 Pre-Intent
"Can people find you at all?"
Awareness Audit
Before someone even needs a lawyer — have they already absorbed your brand? This audit examines TV, billboards, sponsorships, community presence, and social following. The air cover. We scan your social media accounts and available traditional media presence to surface your strengths and blind spots.

02 Brand Strength
"When they find you, what do they think?"
Perception Audit
Reputation, brand sentiment, review quality, website feel, messaging — a firm could be everywhere but still feel sketchy or generic. This audit analyzes how your brand lands with prospective clients: what signals trust, what raises doubt, and what's invisible entirely.

03 Active Search
"When they're actively shopping, do you win?"
Consideration Audit
SEO rankings, paid search, LSAs, content depth, conversion rate optimization — the moment of intent where someone is actively comparing options. This audit reveals what's preventing buyers from seeing you first on Google, and what's stopping them from trusting you can deliver.

04 Operational
"Can you actually capitalize on the leads?"
Readiness Audit
Intake process, CRM, call tracking, speed to lead, case management tech, follow-up sequences. It doesn't matter how visible you are if the phone rings six times and goes to voicemail. This audit benchmarks you against leading law firms and identifies the tools, systems, personnel, and agencies you need to reach the next level.

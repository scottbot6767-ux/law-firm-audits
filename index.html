The Framework

Most firms are optimizing the wrong thing.

They pour budget into Google Ads while their intake is broken. Or they fix their website while nobody can find them. These audits isolate the actual bottleneck — so you fix the right problem first.

You Can't Fix What You Can't See

Most law firms don't know which phase of the buyer journey is costing them the most. They assume it's visibility. Often it's trust. Sometimes it's the phone call that never gets answered. The audit framework surfaces exactly where the gap is — not where you think it is.

Benchmarked Against Elite Firms

Results aren't generic scores — they're calibrated against law firms that have actually cracked client acquisition. You'll know exactly where you stand, and what the gap between you and the top firms in your market actually looks like.

Built for Law Firms Specifically

These aren't adapted marketing audits. Every signal, benchmark, and recommendation is built around the specific dynamics of legal — intake expectations, review platforms, LSAs, referral networks, and the trust requirements that separate legal from every other industry.
